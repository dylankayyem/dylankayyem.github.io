About Me

Hi, I'm Dylan Kayyem, a former Psychology & Philosophy graduate from the University of Colorado turned User Experience designer. At the beginning of January 2021, during the harsh reality of the Covid-19 epidemic, I made a pivotal career decision.

I decided to change my career path because I had found my calling to be a UX Designer. I have always been passionate about technology but never applied myself 100% to the field & had always focused more on social sciences.

After I graduated from the University of Colorado with two bachelor degrees, I planned on pursuing social work but upon further research discovered that a career in product design would allow me to do just this and much more. So with confidence and overwhelming passion, I enrolled in a 3-month boot camp at Brainstation. The experience enhanced my ambition ten-fold and gave me an educated understanding of how I can change the world as a product designer. As a designer, I can help others by empathizing with my users in a perfect combination of research methodology, human psychology & modern philosophy. I am eager to apply these skills in a position as a UX/UI Designer so that I can create ethical solutions to real world problems & enhance the world as we experience it subjectively.

When i'm not obsessing over making my design's pixel perfect, I also love playing with my dog, Moose. You can check out his fun & exciting dog life on his @mooseforprez2020

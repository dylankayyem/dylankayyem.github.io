My Work

PawPrints

January - March, 2021

A social media app for dog-lovers.

The PawPrints App is a passion project that started as a Capstone Project for my Brainstation UX diploma. This is a case study for my social media app for dogs. The study overviews my design process & explains the methodology in developing a solution to a real world problem.

it's a start

March 24th - 25th, 2021

A healthcare support group to give users access to professionals.

It's A Start App was a collaborative project for my team's submission for a Google Hackathon event hosted by Brainstation in March, 2021. This study overviews the final project and highlights the collaborative components of the design process.

Presto

February 9th - 16th, 2021

A heuristic evaluation of Presto (a Canadian Transit App).

The Presto App was a collaborative heuristic project for a sprint - design challenge hosted by Brainstation. The challenge had us redesign a current app and find heuristics that stand out, explain why they don't work, and redesign a successful solution. The study highlights my design process and my contributions to the final product we developed.
